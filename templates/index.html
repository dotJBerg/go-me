About Me

Hi! I’m Jarrett Goldberg, a programmer with a love for technology. Whether it’s code, computers, or cool new electronic I am always fascinated in learning more. I’ve always known I wanted to be a programmer, and I’m grateful to be living that dream today!

Outside of coding, I have a deep love for video games, movies, and going to the theater. There’s something special about experiencing a film on the big screen, and I try to do it as often as I can. I’m also fascinated by space. Whether it’s reading about the cosmos, watching space documentaries, or just staring up at the night sky, I find it endlessly inspiring.

When I do step away from my screen, I enjoy hiking and traveling. My favorite city so far has been Seattle—I love its mix of nature and city life. It also has a wide variety of amazing food. And when it comes to non-city destinations, Bar Harbor, Maine, holds a special place in my heart. There’s nothing quite like the views along the coastline. I also love reading. My favorite book tends to change depending on what I’ve read recently, but if I had to choose, Kafka on the Shore by Haruki Murakami and Do Androids Dream of Electric Sheep? by Philip K. Dick stand out as all-time favorites.

This blog is just a space for me to write about whatever interests me—mostly tech-related topics, but who knows? Maybe I’ll write about a book, a movie, or a travel experience at some point. If you’ve made it this far, thanks for reading!
